About Prime Electrical

Started by an electrician who was sick of call-out cowboys.

Prime Electrical Services was set up in 2011 by Tom Whitfield after ten years working for bigger contractors around the region. He kept seeing the same thing - jobs quoted low then padded out, work left half-certified, customers left in the dark about what they were actually paying for. So he started his own outfit with one rule: explain the job, price it fairly, and do it right.

These days it's a small team of five qualified electricians covering domestic and light commercial work across Millbrook and the surrounding towns. We still turn up in the same vans, still hand-write the same clear invoices, and still test everything before we leave - because a job's not finished until the certificate is.

  • Every job tested and certified to BS 7671 wiring regulations
  • Written quotes with no hidden extras added later
  • Same electrician sees your job through from quote to sign-off
